Spiderman Song cover

Project Description: This is a song cover that I created inspired by Spiderman's movie created using a series of photo manipulation and editing through Photoshop. I didn't have a specific song in mind while creating this but I wanted to capture an ending scene which usually comes in a gloomy mood for spiderman movies

Shang portrait

Project Description: This piece was created using Photoshop which represents a self-portrait. The polar bear is called "Volibear" referencing my favorite character in the video game League Of Legends. He is a thunder god which I chose a blue atmosphere to match and the figure in the piece is a photo of mines that I took to match the scene.

Tjay Glitch

Project Description: This is my glitch art project where I merged a series of photos to generate different glitched colors and effects onto a music artist that I personally love. His name is lil Tjay and he creates hip hop music.

Calmness

Project Description: Calmness is a piece I created through photo manipulation using free stock images to capture a calming scene.

Calmness
